Vice President Internal Drew Hendricks

Hey everybody!

My name is Drew Hendricks and I’m the MACSS VP of Internal Affairs for the upcoming 2016-2017 academic year. I can’t wait to meet everybody, and I look forward to seeing everyone at this year’s social events (BBQs, Holiday Events, Crawls, and the Winter Wine and Cheese)! I have personally never been a part of a MACSS occasion that wasn’t academically enlightening, helpful, or just a great time altogether - and our goal is that all students in attendance feel the same way.

If you have any questions, or would like to help in any capacity (#WeLoveNewFriends), please feel free to drop by the MACSS office or message one of us personally!

-Drew
