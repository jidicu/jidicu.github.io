Vice President Athletics Sara Wang

Hey Anatomy!

My name is Sara and I am so excited to be your VP Athletics this year! I’m from the great city of Winnipeg, home to five-month winters, mosquito-ridden summers and our biggest pride and joy, the Winnipeg Jets. Needless to say, I’m loving my time at McGill and Montreal.

As VP Athletics, my role is to make your experience as an Anatomy student as enjoyable as possible. I’ll be organizing interdepartmental sports games, fun nights at Gerts and much more! Feel free to chat whenever you see me around or send me an email at macss.athletics.@gmail.com if you have any ideas. I look forward to meeting all of you this year!

-Sara
