President Jill Laurin

Hi, I’m Jill and I began my nomadic life in Cold Lake, Alberta, by far the most happening place in Canada. After bouncing around the globe, I completed high school in Albuquerque, New Mexico, and set my sights on McGill due to its reputation and the fact that it rhymes with my name. I have since become enchanted by the vibrant, culturally rich, and radical city that is Montréal. Some of my favourite things include: bagels, learning about people, pretending I can speak French, exploring (whether in hiking boots or strapped to a snowboard), and of course, tequila anatomy.

Starting as a U1 Representative for MACSS, I was thrilled to make announcements about free pizza and BEvERages during ANAT 261 and 262, as well as the occasional wine and cheese pun (it was a gouda time).

As I enter my (hopefully) final year as an Anatomy and Cell Biology undergrad, I am beyond eager to work towards MACSS-imizing the student experience in our department (last pun, I promise).

So, swing by the MACSS office or shoot me a message, I would love to discuss anything and everything, and hear about your own experiences.

-Jill
