NTCs

NTCs are note-packs that can be purchased for specific courses. Created by students enrolled in the course, NTCs are a strong supplementary material for your studies.

MACSS NTCs are sold as weekly sets containing the notes for one week of lectures. The profits are used to fund MACSS events and scholarships. More information will be posted once NTC sales begin!

We sell NTCs for the following Fall 2016 courses:

  • ANAT 261
  • ANAT 321
  • ANAT 365
  • ANAT 381

Pricing:

  • $40 for 1 semester of NTCs
  • $25 for a half-semester (pre- or post-midterm)
