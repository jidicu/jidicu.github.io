Vice President Finance Chloe Hille

Hi Anatomy and friends of Anatomy!

My name is Chloe - I first joined Mcgill Anatomy and Cell Biology Student Society last year as one of the two VP Academics, and this year am excited to take on the role of VP Finance!

My new position entails creating a well-rounded budget that will allow Anatomy students to have an enriched experience by providing them with opportunities to build relationships with peers, administrators and professors, and also to eat good food and have fun while doing so! My role also entails managing funds to hire an excellent staff of NTC writers and editors, and provide scholarships for students in Anatomy and Cell Biology. You can reach me at macss.vpfinance@gmail.com.
