Scholarships

John Bergeron Summer Research Scholarship

For any aspiring summer researchers, MACSS is now accepting applications for the John Bergeron Summer Research Scholarship! The scholarship is open to all students in the Department of Anatomy and Cell Biology and gives you the opportunity to receive funding for a summer research project. All students interested in research are encouraged to apply. The application is due March 10th, 2017 and can be dropped off to the MACSS office. Any questions about the scholarship can be directed to macss.presidents@gmail.com.

MACSS Scholarship

The MACSS Scholarship application for 2017 is now open! The application process is competition-based, aimed at recognizing and promoting student extracurricular involvement and achievement. There are three $400 Scholarships which will be awarded to one Anatomy and Cell Biology student in each U1, U2, and U3. Attached are the instructions on what you need to do to in order to be eligible to win one of the scholarships! Applications are due by March 10th, 2017 and can be dropped off to the MACSS office. Any questions about the scholarship can be directed to macss.presidents@gmail.com.
