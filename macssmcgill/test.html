What are NTCs?

NTCs are note-packs that can be purchased for specific courses. Created by students enrolled in the course, NTCs are a strong supplementary material for your studies. MACSS NTCs are sold as weekly sets containing the notes for one week of lectures. The profits are used to fund MACSS events and scholarships. See below for a sample from ANAT 365.
