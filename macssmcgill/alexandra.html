Vice President Communications Alexandra Tinfow

Hi guys! My name is Allie Tinfow and I will be your VP Communications for the 2016-2017 year. I’m from the US (New Jersey specifically) and I’m going into my fourth year at McGill. When I’m not taking notes at council meetings, sending out listservs, or getting the ANAT services orders together, I’m playing soccer for the McGill Martlets. I’ve been playing soccer for a long time and absolutely love it!

This year will be my third year on council. I was VP Communications last year, and before that I was a U1 Representative. I loved sending out colorful listservs and picking out Anat clothes so much last year that I wanted to do it again! I look forward to an awesome year with all of you!

-Alexandra
