3D Models

MACSS offers 3D-printed anatomical models for rental and sale for studying and collection purposes. Stunning state of the art and anatomically correct replicas of the heart, brain, and more! Fill out the order form and complete your payment in person at the MACSS office. They're sold on a first come, first serve basis, so get your orders in soon!

Rentals

Fees are $1.00 for a 24-hour rental period. You may rent a maximum of 2 models for up to two days consecutively if the model has not been requested.

A late fee of $5.00 will be charged for each day beyond the 24-hour rental period.
