About Us

Who We Are

MACSS is your Student Council for Anatomy and Cell Biology. We're here to take you to greater heights in and outside of the classroom!

Our Locale

The MACSS Office is nestled in the lobby of the picturesque Strathcona Anatomy and Dentistry Building, the home of our department since 1911.

The Program

B.Sc. Anatomy and Cell Biology students receive instruction from world-class researchers to build a strong understanding of human anatomy and biology, from the cellular to the organ level.

The Department

Founded c. 1829, our department has always been home to cutting-edge discoveries and groundbreaking scientists, especially today.
