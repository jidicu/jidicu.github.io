U1 Representative Alper Avi Benardete

Hello Everyone!

My name is Alper Avi Benardete (just call me Avi)! I am one of three U1 representatives of MACSS this year. This is my second year at McGill and in Canada! I was born and lived my whole life in Istanbul, Turkey. Turkish Delight became my nickname here. ;)

I’ve always loved being involved in student councils to help, improve and embellish the community I belong to. This year I, and the rest of the MACSS council, are here to solve any problems you may have and make this year unforgettable. Come say hi and chat with me whenever you see me on campus! You can find me in all the U1 classes (ANAT 261, PHGY 209, BIOL 200...) If I’m not inside a lab or classroom I might be laying on Lower Field, wandering around St. Laurent, chilling at the MACSS office or skiing on a Saturday! You can also reach me at u1.macss@gmail.com.

Looking forward to getting to know all of you amazing Anat People!

-Avi
