Vice President External Michaela Sadinsky

Hi! My name is Michaela, and I originate from our bumping capital, Ottawa, Ontario, and yes, I can see Parliament from my window! After taking a gap year after high school, I came to McGill, and spent the first year hiking up the hill to Upper Rez (preventing that Freshman 15, right?)...

Once I made my way back down the mountain to 2nd year, I entered the world of MACSS as a U1 Rep. I enjoyed coming up with TOO MANY puns to throw into announcements in class!

3rd year came with new classes and a new role, as VP Athletics. The irony of connecting me with that position is beyond hilarious. Although a fan of the McGill gym, athletics and I pair like Montreal winters and sandals. Planning social events and interdepartmental competitions was still a blast, and opened my eyes to the opportunities with MACSS. To get deep, it also taught me that a position is what you make of it, and you can learn a lot about yourself when thrown into new situations.

Now, entering into my final year, I come to you as VP External, in charge of coordinating sponsorship and scholarship opportunities, and getting Anatomy with the greater McGill and Montreal communities. So, come by the office to chat, shoot me a message, or stop me in the middle of the library to say hi! Looking forward to getting to know as many of you as possible, and making your time in ANAT the best it can be! Also, check out this pic of me looking as McGill as it gets!

-Michaela
